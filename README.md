# Credit-Card-Financial-Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **credit card customer financial data** to understand customer spending behavior, transaction patterns, credit utilization, and potential credit risk indicators. The analysis was performed using **SQL for data preparation** and **Power BI for data visualization and dashboard creation**.

The goal of the project is to provide a **data-driven financial dashboard** that helps banks and financial institutions gain insights into customer activity, identify high-value customers, detect risk patterns, and support better financial decision-making.

---

# Problem Statement

Financial institutions handle a large volume of credit card transactions daily. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to understand:

* Customer spending behavior
* Credit card utilization patterns
* Payment method preferences
* Credit risk indicators
* Customer demographic impact on revenue

The objective of this project is to **build an interactive financial dashboard** that provides transparency into credit card operations and enables stakeholders to monitor revenue, transactions, and customer financial behavior effectively.

# Tools & Technologies Used

* **SQL** – Database creation and data loading

# Dataset Description

The dataset contains two main tables:

### Customer Data

Includes customer demographic information such as:

* Client Number
* Age
* Gender
* Income
* Education Level
* Marital Status
* Occupation
* Customer Satisfaction Score
* State

### Credit Card Transaction Data

Includes financial and transaction related fields such as:

* Card Category
* Credit Limit
* Transaction Amount
* Transaction Count
* Expenditure Type
* Interest Earned
* Payment Mode (Chip / Swipe / Online)
* Credit Utilization Ratio
* Delinquent Accounts

# Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard contains **three analytical sections**:

### 1. Executive Financial Overview

Displays high-level KPIs including:

* Total Revenue
* Total Interest Earned
* Total Transactions
* Average Credit Utilization

Visualizations used:

* Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
* Revenue by Card Category (Column Chart)
* Revenue by Expenditure Type (Donut Chart)
* Revenue by Payment Mode (Bar Chart)

---

### 2. Customer Insights Analysis

Analyzes which customer segments contribute most to revenue.

Visualizations include:

* Revenue by Age Group
* Revenue by Gender
* Revenue by Income Group
* Revenue by Customer Job
* Revenue by Education Level
* Revenue by State (Map Visualization)

---

### 3. Transaction & Risk Analysis

Focuses on financial behavior and credit risk patterns.

Visualizations include:

* Credit Limit vs Balance (Scatter Plot)
* Quarterly Revenue vs Transaction Count (Combo Chart)
* Delinquent Accounts by Card Category
* Risk Level Distribution

# Key Insights from the Analysis

### 1. Card Category Contribution

The **Blue credit card category generated the highest revenue**, indicating it is the most widely used card among customers.

---

### 2. Spending Behavior

Customers spend most of their credit card revenue in categories such as:

* Bills
* Entertainment
* Grocery

This indicates that credit cards are primarily used for **essential expenses and lifestyle spending**.

---

### 3. Customer Segments Driving Revenue

Customers working in **business and white-collar professions contribute the highest revenue**, indicating higher spending power within these groups.

---

### 4. Payment Method Preference

The majority of transactions are performed using **Swipe and Chip payment modes**, while **online transactions contribute a smaller share**.

---

### 5. Credit Utilization Risk

Customers with **high credit utilization ratios show increased delinquency risk**, which may require monitoring from financial institutions.

---

# Business Recommendations

### 1. Promote Premium Credit Cards

Banks can encourage high-income customers to upgrade to **Gold or Platinum cards** to increase transaction value.

### 2. Personalized Marketing Campaigns

Offer targeted promotions for high-spending categories like **groceries, travel, and entertainment**.

### 3. Credit Risk Monitoring

Customers with **high credit utilization and delinquent accounts** should be monitored to reduce financial risk.

### 4. Increase Digital Payment Adoption

Encourage customers to use **online payment methods** by offering cashback or reward incentives.
